Uncle Cha's

Simple, cheap-ish, and good Cantonese noodle shop located right next to the famous Cha's diner. Are they related? Somehow, but we couldn't get a straight answer. Something seems to have gone awry... But that doesn't matter -- the noodles are good. The menu is simple; just choose dry noodles or soup noodles, the kind of noodle, and your meat. You can get veg on the side. Aside from a few drinks, that's it. You're looking at 28-50rmb per person. Definitely not on the level as similar shops in HK, but for Shanghai, they get close enough. No, you cannot order food from Cha's and bring it in here.
